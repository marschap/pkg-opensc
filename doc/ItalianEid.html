Italian Infocamere

OpenSC 0.9.6 supports type 1202 and 1203 of the Italian Infocamere card. Support for type 1400 and 1600 can be found in the lastest snapshots (http://www.opensc.org/files/snapshots/) and will be inserted in the upcoming 0.10 release.
