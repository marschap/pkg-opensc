German ID Cards

Germany has several laws for smart cards, and most ID cards conforming to those laws are using the TCOS 2.0X card operating system.

A SignTrust card was successfully tested with OpenSC 0.10.0-beta. Pkcs11-tool will fail on the signature only key, because it tries to do raw rsa for signing and the key does not support that. Applications should be fine, unless they try raw rsa padding.

You can find more information about how do this here.

German eHBA, eGK

In 2006 all german physicians and apothecaries will be equipped with a smartcard, the so called eHBA (elektronischer Heilberufeausweis). After that all german citizens that are a member of a public health insurance company (gesetzliche Krankenkasse) will get a similar card, the so called eGK (elektronische Gesundheitskarte). This means that virtually every german citizen will have a smartcard soon.
